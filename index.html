üéÆ What is Avenged MVP?

Avenged MVP 6.1 is a comprehensive basketball gaming script designed for Cronus Zen devices. It provides automated shooting mechanics, dribble moves, defensive assists, and various quality-of-life improvements to enhance your gameplay experience.

This script is compatible with both PlayStation and Xbox controllers. The OLED display provides real-time feedback and menu navigation.

üèÄ Shooting Features

üéØ Button Rhythm (Page 1)

The core shooting feature that converts Square/X button presses into optimized right-stick movements for consistent shot timing.

How It Works:

  • Press Square to initiate shot
  • Script converts to RS down movement
  • On release, performs smooth flick up
  • Includes random variation for anti-detection
Default: ON
This feature respects the remap button setting - if you have Square mapped to L3/R3, using those buttons will trigger normal Square behavior instead.
‚è±Ô∏è Tempo (Page 2)

Controls the delay (in milliseconds) before the flick-up motion executes after releasing the shot button.

0ms (Fastest) 42ms (Default) 300ms (Slowest)
Range: 0 - 300 ms | Default: 42ms

Recommended Values:

  • 0-30ms: Very fast releases, for quick-twitch shooters
  • 30-60ms: Balanced timing, works for most builds
  • 60-100ms: Slower, more deliberate shots
üî• Turbo Shots (Page 3)

Determines the shooting style when holding R2/RT while shooting.

Hop Jumper Turbo Fade ‚úì

Hop Jumper Mode:

Flicks RS DOWN when holding R2 + shooting. Best for quick hop shots and catch-and-shoot situations.

Turbo Fade Mode (Default):

Flicks RS UP when holding R2 + shooting. Ideal for fadeaways, step-backs, and creating separation.

Turbo Shot Activation Hold R2 + Square
üïπÔ∏è RS Shooting (Page 4)

Enables shooting using the right stick instead of the Square button. Detects stick holds and performs appropriate flick motion on release.

Default: OFF

How It Works:

  • Hold RS down/up for 300ms+
  • Release to trigger automatic flick
  • Direction depends on initial stick position
  • Disabled during menu navigation
Hold Duration Required: 300ms
RS Shooting is disabled when L2 is held with R1 or D-Pad buttons to prevent conflicts with menu navigation.
‚ö° Automated Shot (Page 5)

One-button instant shot that automatically holds and releases for you. Perfect for catch-and-shoot situations.

Default: ON
Trigger Automated Shot R3 (if Remap = L3) or L3 (if Remap = R3)

Uses the opposite button of your remap setting. If Square is remapped to L3, then R3 triggers Automated Shot.

üõë QuickStop (Page 9)

Automatically cancels your momentum and initiates a shot when pressing Square while moving. Creates space for clean jumpers.

OFF AUTO ‚úì LT/L2

Mode Descriptions:

  • OFF: QuickStop disabled
  • AUTO: Triggers when holding R2 + moving + pressing Square
  • LT/L2: Triggers when holding L2 + moving + pressing Square

What QuickStop Does:

  1. Cancels Square input
  2. Zeros left stick (stops movement)
  3. Flicks RS right briefly (50ms)
  4. Holds RS down while you hold Square
  5. On release, performs full flick-up shot

üõ°Ô∏è Defense Features

üéØ Defense Assist (Page 15)

Automatically mirrors your left stick movement to the right stick when on defense, helping you stay in front of your opponent.

Default: ON

Activation:

Activate Defense Assist L2 + R1 + Move Left Stick

How It Works:

  • Hold L2 (defensive stance) + R1 (intense-D)
  • Move left stick in any direction
  • Right stick automatically follows left stick
  • R1 input is consumed (not sent to game)
This is incredibly useful for staying in front of dribblers. Your right stick (contest/hands) will track your movement direction automatically.
üèÄ Dunk Meter (Page 14)

Automatically holds the right stick down when attempting dunks to help time the dunk meter.

Default: OFF

Activation:

Activate Dunk Meter L1 + R2

How It Works:

  • Hold L1 (lob/dunk modifier) + R2 (sprint)
  • Script automatically holds RS down at 100%
  • Also keeps R2 at 100% for maximum drive
  • Releases when you release L1 or R2
  • 200ms grace period after releasing R2
When Dunk Meter is active, other RS-based shooting features are temporarily disabled to prevent conflicts.

‚öôÔ∏è Utility Features

‚ö° Unlimited Stamina (Page 16)

Reduces your left stick input intensity to 70% to conserve stamina while still moving at a reasonable pace.

Default: OFF

How It Works:

  • Left stick values are multiplied by 70%
  • Applies when stick movement > 20 units
  • Small movements (< 20) are zeroed out
  • Disabled during dunk attempts
Movement Throttle: 70% of max speed
This is great for conserving energy during long possessions or when you need to stay fresh for late-game situations.

üéÆ Playstyle Tips for Centers:

  • Pick & Pop: Set screens and pop to the perimeter. Use R3 for instant catch-and-shoot.
  • Corner Spacing: When your PG drives, drift to the corner. Automated Shot makes you deadly here.
  • Post Fades: Use Turbo Fade (R2 + Square) when backing down smaller defenders.
  • Elbow Game: The free throw line extended is your sweet spot. QuickStop helps here.
  • Defensive Balance: You still need to protect the rim - Defense Assist helps you stay in position.
Don't forget you're still a Center! Even with shooting abilities, you need to crash the boards and contest shots. Keep Dunk Meter ON for putbacks.
üìù General Tips for All Positions
  • Start with defaults: The script's default settings are well-tuned. Only change what you need.
  • Use profiles: Create separate profiles for different builds or game modes.
  • Fine-tune tempo: If shots feel early, increase tempo. If late, decrease it.
  • Practice timing: Even with automation, you need to learn when to trigger shots.
  • Defense Assist is universal: Almost every build benefits from this feature.
  • Don't over-dribble: Even with automated moves, excessive dribbling kills stamina.
Remember to save your settings by pressing Circle/B before starting a game. Settings only persist when properly saved!
